Mess, Managed

Tour d'horizon

No more manual headaches. Just intelligence that works.

Mess, Managed is a research-driven Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) platform designed to automate and visualize Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs) for tracking Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs). Using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and the MITRE ATT&CK framework, the platform extracts insights from unstructured threat intelligence reports, eliminating the need for manual documentation.
